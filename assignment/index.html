TikTok's Meteoric Rise

TikTok's ascent in the world of social media has been nothing short of spectacular. As a platform that champions creativity and spontaneity, it has captivated millions worldwide. But what does the data tell us about this phenomenon? The density plots shed light on pivotal metrics: followers, views, likes, and comments. At a glance, these visualizations offer a snapshot of the typical range for each metric, painting a picture of user engagement and content virality on TikTok. Interestingly, the similar shapes under each chart hint at a deeper connection.
